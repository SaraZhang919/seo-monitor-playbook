# Changelog

All notable changes to the SEO Monitoring Playbook are documented here.

Format: `## [version] — YYYY-MM-DD` followed by a brief description of what changed and why.

---

## [1.0.0] — 2025-05-13

### Initial release

**Daily tab — 13 items across 4 categories:**

Traffic & visibility
- Organic sessions vs yesterday & 7-day avg
- Impressions & clicks in GSC (28-day rolling)
- Average position for top 20 keywords
- CTR for pages in positions 1–5
- AI Overview (SGE) presence for target queries

Technical health
- Site uptime & server response time
- New crawl errors in GSC Coverage report
- Core Web Vitals status (LCP, INP, CLS)
- New 404 errors & internal broken links
- Sitemap accessibility & submission status

Backlinks & authority
- New backlink alerts — last 24 hours
- Lost backlink alerts — last 24 hours
- Brand mentions — linked & unlinked

Content & indexing
- New content from yesterday — confirm indexed
- Top 5 pages — ranking & traffic health check
- Crawl budget efficiency (AI site priority)

**Weekly tab — 16 items across 4 categories:**

Rankings deep dive
- Full keyword ranking report — week-over-week
- Competitor ranking movements for shared keywords
- Keywords on positions 11–20 — quick win targets
- People Also Ask & featured snippet opportunities
- AI Overview citations — are you included?

Technical audit
- Weekly crawl of new & updated pages
- Core Web Vitals field data — all pages
- Schema/structured data validation
- Internal linking audit for new content

Content performance
- Pages with declining CTR — title/meta optimization
- AI-generated content engagement metrics
- Content freshness — flag pages needing update

Backlink profile
- Weekly link acquisition summary
- Toxic/spammy backlinks — disavow management

**Monthly tab — 16 items across 4 categories:**

Full technical SEO audit
- Comprehensive site crawl — structure & health
- E-E-A-T signals comprehensive review
- Index coverage full report & ratio analysis
- Log file analysis — Googlebot crawl behavior

Strategy & content
- Keyword research refresh & new opportunities
- Content gap analysis vs top 3 competitors
- E-E-A-T enhancement — expert signals & citations
- Underperforming content audit & refresh plan

Monthly reporting
- Month-over-month organic performance report
- Keyword ranking distribution analysis
- Domain authority trend & link profile health
- Brand search volume & E-E-A-T growth

**Features:**
- Expandable detail panel per item with Why / Signals / Actions
- localStorage persistence keyed to current date (auto-resets daily)
- Progress tracking per tab (counter cards + progress bar)
- Category collapse/expand
- Manual "Reset tab" button
- Dark mode via `prefers-color-scheme`
- Mobile responsive
